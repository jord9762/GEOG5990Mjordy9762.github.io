Population of West Yorkshire major settlements with big city markers

My index page

The map above displays 5 major settlements around West Yorkshire. Settlements include, Leeds, Bradford, Kirklees, Calderdale and Wakefield. Leeds is the largest settlement out of the 5 displayed with a population of 715402 and the smallest settlement is Calderdale with a population of 192405. Markers were downloaded via the mapicons.mapmarker icon hub. The populations for major settlements were taken from the UK data service website.
