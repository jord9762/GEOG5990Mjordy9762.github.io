Welcome to the first installment of GY20JCR's Interactive History. The aim of Interactive History is to help teach people of all ages about key historical events in a more digestable and engaging way. Furthermore the presentation of information on a map is helpful for understanding geographical context for information displayed which is difficult when engaging with conventional literature.

This iteration will be covering key events of the First,Second,Third and Fourth Crusades which were a series of religious conflicts ranging from 1096 to 1204. For a visual representation of sites presented please visit the Imagery tab or if you would like to add locations you think were also important during this time period please visit the Add marker tab.

The map above is composed of 39 markers representing key cities and landmarks present throughout the crusader era. References for displayed information can be found within the supporting submission document. Please be aware that some of the historical content may not be appropriate for very young children and therefore information should be screened beforehand.
